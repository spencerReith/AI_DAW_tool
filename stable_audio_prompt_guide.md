# StableAudio Prompt Structure Guide

## Get More From Your Text Prompts

Get more from your audio creations by adding structure to your prompts.

There are a few ways you can structure your prompts using **core style and genre**, **key musical elements**, **mood and emotion**, **specific details**, **BPM**, and **additional instructions**. The order matters.

> **Good:** A Deep House anthem (genre) with lush synthesizers (elements), featuring a euphoric mood (emotion) at 125 BPM (technical)
>
> **Less Effective:** Make something with synths that's happy and danceable with some drums and bass at 125

---

## Prompt Elements

Here's an overview of the elements you can describe when creating a prompt:

| Element | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Format** | Solo, Band, Orchestra, Chorus, Duet |
| **Genre** | Rock, Pop, Hip Hop, Indie, Foley, RnB |
| **Sub-genre** | Drum loops, Electric guitar, Pop Music, Chillout, Ambient, Techno |
| **Instruments** | Piano, Drum machine, Synthesizer, Snare drum, Keyboard, Organ, Strings, Percussion, Ukelele |
| **Moods** | Dramatic, Inspiring, Magical, Uplifting, Driving, Animated, Atmospheric, Happy |
| **Styles** | Film Instrumental, 2000s, 1960s, Dance, Video Games, High Tech, Sci-Fi |
| **Tempo** | Medium, Slow, Building, Fast, Very Fast |
| **BPM** | 180 (Drum & Bass), 140 (Dubstep), 120 (Techno/Trance), 100 (House), 90 (Hip-Hop), 60 (Dub) |

## Prompt Structures

### Format: Solo — Stems

Use `Format: Solo` for isolated stems.

1. `Format: Solo | Instruments: Vibraphone`
2. `Format: Solo | Genre: Pop | Atmospheric guitar`
3. `Format: Solo | Instruments: Drums, Bass, 808 bass stabs | Moods: Euphoric, Feel-Good | BPM: 100`

### Format: Band — Full Melodies & Instrumentals

Use `Format: Band` for full melodies and instrumentals.

1. `Format: Band | Genre: Pop | Subgenre: Energizing Music`
2. `Format: Band | Genre: Rock | Subgenre: Indie Rock | Instruments: echoing electric guitars with chorus, well recorded drum-kit, Electric Bass, occasional soaring harmonies | Moving, Epic, Climactic | BPM: 125`
3. `Format: Band | Genre: Drum & Bass | Subgenre: Liquid D&B | Instruments: Sustained Piano, Reese Bass, Vocal Chops, Drum Kit, Brass Hits | Moods: Spacious, Atmospheric, Ethereal | BPM: 170`

### Genre & Subgenre

1. `Genre: UK Bass | Instruments: 707 Drum Machine, Strings, 808 bass stabs, Beautiful Synths`
2. `Genre: Rock | Subgenre: Pop Rock, Indie Rock | Instruments: Guitar, Drum Kit, Bass, Electric Guitar | Moods: Driving, Strong, Edgy | Tempo: Medium`
3. `Genre: Hip Hop | Subgenre: Hip Hop Instrumental, Lo-Fi | Instruments: Drum Machine, Synthesizer, Synth stabs, Bass | Moods: Groovy, Mystical, Up-lifting, Flowing | BPM: 90`
4. `Genre: Pop | Subgenre: Electronic | Instruments: Drum Machine, Bass, Lush Synthesizer Pads, Synthesizer Arp, Synth Bass | Moods: Honest, Heart-Felt, Melancholic, Vibe, Cool, Modern | BPM: 120`

## Simple to Complex Prompts

Simple prompts are good for **clean output audio**. Complex prompts are good for adding **texture and depth**.

### Simple

1. `Format: Solo | Instruments: Drums`
2. `Format: Band | Subgenre: Chillout | Moods: Vintage, Soul`
3. `Format: Solo | Instruments: beautiful piano arpeggio`

### Medium

1. `Instruments: synthesizer arpeggio, beautiful rhodes piano chords and melodies, epic sweeping string section, syncopated percussion | Moods: mysterious, mystical, climactic, atmospheric`
2. `Format: Orchestra | Subgenre: Hollywood Orchestral Epic | Instruments: Brass section, Piano, Bass, Vocal Chops, Strings | Moods: Spacious, Atmospheric, Ethereal`
3. `Format: Band | Subgenre: Soulful Boom Bap Hip Hop | Instruments: solemn effected piano, SP-1200, low-key swing drums, sine wave bass | Moods: characterful, peaceful, Interesting, well-arranged composition | 90 BPM`
4. `Format: Band | Subgenre: Post Rock | Instruments: echoing electric guitars with chorus, well recorded drum-kit, electric bass, occasional soaring harmonies | Moods: moving, epic, climactic | 125 BPM`

### Complex

1. `Format: Orchestra | Subgenre: Hollywood Orchestral Epic | Instruments: Strings, Drum Kit, Electric Bass, Choir, String Section, Flute, Harp | Moods: Atmospheric, Spacious, cinematic, Inspiring, Beautiful | Styles: Recording, Auditorium, Film Instrumental | Tempo: Medium`
2. `Genre: Hip Hop | Subgenre: 70s Vintage Funk | Instruments: Hip Hop instrumental, Solemn effected Piano, SP-1200, low-key swing drums, sine wave bass, MPC Drums, Moog Synthesizer Lead, Rhodes Piano, Vintage Drum Machine, Short 808 Kick Stabs for Bass, Brass Stabs, String Section | Moods: Inspiring, Beautiful, Up-lifting, Epic, Flowing | BPM: 90`
3. `Genre: Trap | Styles: well arranged | Instruments: tap delay marimba arp, Shaker, Rim shot, 808 Kick, Syncopated Percussion, lush synthesizer pads, reverberating bongos, modular synth arp, granular synthesized | Moods: Ethereal, Transcendent, Atmospheric, Hypnotic | BPM: 130`
